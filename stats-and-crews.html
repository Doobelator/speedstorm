How does it work ?


Maximize your racer's performance by selecting the ideal crew with this calculator. Choose up to four crew members, each with a star rating from one to five, to see the total stat boost provided. This feature helps in determining the best crew combinations for your racing style, allowing for strategic planning to leverage your crew's strengths effectively on the track.


This tool is optimized for estimating your total stats with any crew selected for the character. Select the crews (max 4), then select the stars and see the resulting stats! It was never easier to calculate on which crews you want to invest.


Additional tips and tricks: Each racer has a hidden topspeed cap, meaning that only going for topspeed, will not benefit you in the long run.



Due to limited available data, the calculator shows a close estimation of the stats. The calculation behind this might put some stats a little off, especially around level 50. Come visit the discord and share your thoughts!
